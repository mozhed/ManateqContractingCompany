Our Services

Whatever your construction requirements Manateq General Contracting Company can help you - from new builds to refurbishments, conversions and extensions to restoration projects, our professional and experienced team can ensure we provide you with the best possible result. Manateq General Contracting Company employs a team of highly skilled qualified tradesmen and craftsmen who all specialise in their respective trades or crafts. Our team includes Ground workers, Tiler’s ,Bricklayers, Carpenters and Joiners, Roofing specialists, Painters and Decorators, Gas Safe registered Plumbing and Heating engineers, NIC EIC approved Electricians, and Plasterers . We work closely with private homeowners, architects, social housing clients and businesses to ensure projects are delivered on time and budget.
